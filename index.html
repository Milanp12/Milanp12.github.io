Beauty by Anjali

Make-Up, Hair, Styling

I take pride in knowing how to capture and enhance my clients’ natural beauty.

I am a passionate makeup artist and hair stylist based in New Jersey. My love for all things art started at a very early age. I have been drawing and painting since I was young. Soon enough, this passion blossomed into a career and I brought my artwork to life through makeup artistry. While putting myself through college, I worked as a freelance makeup artist at different special occasions such as prom, weddings, birthdays, photoshoots, and many more events. I take pride in knowing how to capture and enhance my clients’ natural beauty. I find it very rewarding to make someone look and feel like the best version of themselves. My journey in the beauty industry and my passion for the field is ever-growing. When I am not doing makeup or hair, I enjoy going to baseball games and concerts.
